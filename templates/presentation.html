The Problem

The navigation instructions given by SatNavs and mobile mapping apps are very different to the way humans give directions to each other.

Introducing Humap

Humap attempts to locate public entities and points of interest to give you useful instructions based upon your surroundings.

Datasources and Technology

Google Directions API - The backbone of the application
SatNav Point of Interest Imports - Our own solution for data
Culturegrid API - Providing us with useful public entities
Google Places API - 'Beefing' out our points of interest
